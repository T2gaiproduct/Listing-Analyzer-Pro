# Amazon Product Title Generation Prompt

Whenever you generate an Amazon Product Title, follow these instructions exactly to create a high-converting, SEO-optimized, and Amazon-compliant title.

## Objective

Generate product titles that:

* Maximize search visibility (SEO)
* Improve click-through rate (CTR)
* Clearly communicate the product
* Look premium and professional
* Are easy to read on desktop and mobile
* Follow Amazon best practices

## Output Requirements

* Generate **1 optimized Amazon title**.
* Keep the title between **150–200 characters** (unless a marketplace requires a different limit).
* Return **only the final title** without explanations, headings, quotation marks, or Markdown.

## Title Structure

Follow this order whenever applicable:

**Brand | Main Product | Primary Keyword | Key Feature | Material | Size/Capacity | Color | Quantity | Compatibility/Use Case | Additional Benefit**

### Example Structure

Brand + Product Type + Main Keyword + Material + Capacity + Key Feature + Color + Pack Size + Ideal Use

---

## Writing Guidelines

### 1. Start with the Brand Name

Always begin with the brand name (if provided).

Example:
Tech2Globe Stainless Steel Water Bottle...

If no brand is provided, start directly with the product name.

---

### 2. Use the Most Important Keywords First

Include the highest-value keywords naturally near the beginning.

Examples:

* Stainless Steel Water Bottle
* Wireless Earbuds
* Air Fryer
* Yoga Mat
* Dog Harness

Avoid keyword stuffing.

---

### 3. Include Only Relevant Features

Include only meaningful selling points, such as:

* Leakproof
* BPA Free
* Vacuum Insulated
* Waterproof
* Foldable
* Heavy Duty
* Rechargeable
* Fast Charging
* Non-Slip

Do not list unnecessary specifications.

---

### 4. Include Product Attributes

Where applicable include:

* Material
* Capacity
* Dimensions
* Size
* Color
* Quantity
* Pack Size
* Model
* Compatibility

Example:
750ml | Black | Pack of 2

---

### 5. Add Use Cases Naturally

Include who or where the product is ideal for.

Examples:
Office, Gym, Travel, Home, Camping, Hiking, Kitchen, School

---

### 6. Readability Rules

Use separators like commas or hyphens naturally.

Example:

Brand Stainless Steel Water Bottle, Vacuum Insulated, Leakproof, BPA Free, 750ml, Black, for Gym, Office & Travel

Avoid excessive punctuation.

---

### 7. Amazon Compliance

Do NOT:

* Use promotional phrases such as:

  * Best
  * #1
  * Guaranteed
  * Cheapest
  * Free Shipping
  * Hot Sale
  * Limited Offer
* Use ALL CAPS (except brand abbreviations if required).
* Use emojis or special characters.
* Repeat keywords unnecessarily.
* Include subjective or unverifiable claims.

---

## SEO Best Practices

Naturally include:

* Primary keyword
* Secondary keyword
* Feature keyword
* Material
* Size
* Use case

Every keyword should fit naturally into the title.

---

## Writing Style

The title should:

* Read naturally like a sentence.
* Prioritize clarity over stuffing keywords.
* Look similar to premium Amazon brands.
* Instantly communicate what the product is and why customers should click.

Always generate a polished, customer-friendly, SEO-rich Amazon title that balances discoverability, readability, and conversion potential.
